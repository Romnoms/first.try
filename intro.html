Glioblastoma: A Metabolically Heterogeneous Malignancy

Clinical Challenges

  • Highly invasive and infiltrative growth pattern
  • Extensive intratumoral heterogeneity
  • Blood-brain barrier limits drug delivery
  • Inevitable recurrence despite aggressive treatment
  • Resistance to standard therapies (TMZ, radiation)

Metabolic Reprogramming

  • Enhanced glycolysis (Warburg effect)
  • Altered lipid metabolism for membrane synthesis
  • Glutamine addiction for biosynthesis
  • Hypoxia-driven metabolic adaptation
  • Metabolic flexibility enabling survival

SCD in Cancer Biology

  • Overexpressed in multiple cancer types

  • Regulates lipid signaling pathways
  • Linked to chemotherapy resistance
