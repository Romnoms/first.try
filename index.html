Analysis Overview

Objective: Visualize drug efficacy in killing DIPG (Diffuse Intrinsic Pontine Glioma) cancer cells across different therapeutic categories.

Data Interpretation: Positive values = Good (% cells killed), Negative values = Bad (cell growth occurred)

Layout: Each heatmap shows drugs as rows and DIPG cell lines (4 & 17) as columns.

Color Coding: Warm colors (Orange/Red) = High cell death (effective) → Cool colors (Blue) = Cell growth (ineffective)

Accessibility: Colorblind-friendly palette with high contrast black text on light backgrounds.
